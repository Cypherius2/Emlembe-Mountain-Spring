Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your delivery areas?

We deliver throughout Eswatini, with same-day service in Mbabane and next-day service in Manzini. Regional areas receive scheduled deliveries 2-3 times per week.

What is your minimum order quantity?

For home delivery, our minimum order is 12 bottles. Corporate and bulk orders have different minimums - contact us for specific requirements.

Do you offer corporate discounts?

Yes! We offer volume discounts for corporate clients, with rates starting at 15% off for orders over 200 bottles per month. Contact us for custom pricing.

Can I schedule regular deliveries?

Absolutely! Our subscription service allows you to schedule automatic deliveries weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. You can modify or cancel anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money, bank transfers, and major credit cards. Corporate clients can also set up monthly billing accounts.

Is your water tested for quality?

Yes, our water undergoes rigorous testing at multiple stages. We test for purity, mineral content, and safety standards, with certifications available upon request.
